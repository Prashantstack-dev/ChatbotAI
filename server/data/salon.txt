Q: How long should my hair be for a perm?
A: For men, 2–4 inches (5–10cm) is ideal. Consult our stylist for best results.

A: Book women’s medium hair service if your hair reaches chest length.

A: Our La Biosthetique scalp spa includes analysis, exfoliation, cleansing, massage, and mist treatment.

Q: How long will a perm last?
A: Typically 3–6 months depending on hair type and maintenance.

Q: How much is hair colour?
A: Prices start from $160 for short hair. Final cost depends on hair length, thickness, and applications.

Q: Can you perm coloured hair?
A: Yes, but not bleached hair. Consultation is recommended.

Q: What is the difference between Straight and Keratin?
A: Straightening is permanent. Keratin smooths hair and lasts 3–6 months.

Q: What’s the difference between Normal Perm and Digital Perm?
A: Normal perm uses chemicals. Digital perm uses heat + chemicals for more natural, longer-lasting curls.

Q: What is a Setting Perm?
A: A setting perm uses higher temperatures to create large waves. Consultation is recommended.
